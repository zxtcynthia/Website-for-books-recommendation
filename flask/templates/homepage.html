ABOUT US

What we do?


We recommend books for you through our database and search engine built by our own. There are more than 17000 books from more than 30 categories for your choice.

How to get the book you want?


Click the search button on the upper right corner, then:

Option 1--Accurate Search

You can type the book name or the unique ISBN (International Standard Book Number), then we will show book details for you!

Option 2--Do not know what to read? We can help you!

We provide several options for you. You can choose book categories, author name, language, price, best seller rank, rating of books (average score), publication year, paperback, customer reviews.

See if you like it.


Once you finish searching/selecting, we will provide a bunch of detailed information about this book such as book title, author, category, language, ISBN, best seller ranking, price, rating distribution, average score of the book, customer reviews, total number of reviews, paperback, introduction of the book and more.

Bad luck? Try one from our recommendations!


If you do not like the book you get, we also provide several books for you based on our algorithm. We take many factors into consideration into account and provide books that have similar properties to the book we just showed you. You can pick can one and start reading!
